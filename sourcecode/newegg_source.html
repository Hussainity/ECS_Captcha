Use of cookies by Newegg

A cookie is a small text file that is generated by a website and is stored on your device. When you access our site, a number of cookies used by Newegg and third parties are sent to your browser and stored on your device. When you next visit our site, Newegg and the third parties will use this information to recognize and remember your device. We use cookies and similar technologies to make our site work and to improve our site and our services. You can manage cookies though your browser’s settings. Please note that some of the uses of the site and the services provided on the site may not function if cookies are disabled. Further information about the cookies and similar technologies we use on the site can be found in our privacy policy. Privacy Policy

Functional Cookies

These cookies are used to provide you with certain functionality on the site. These cookies allow us to analyse how our visitors use the site and monitor the site’s performance. This allows us to provide a high quality experience by customising our offering, and quickly identifying and fixing any issues that arise. The information collected by these cookies is not associated with your personal data by us or by our contractors.
